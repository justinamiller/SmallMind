# ITIL v4 Knowledge Management Practice

## Purpose

Maintain and improve the effective, efficient, and convenient use of knowledge across the organization.

## Key Concepts

**Knowledge**: Information in context, enabling decisions and actions

**Knowledge Base**: Central repository of knowledge articles, FAQs, solutions

**Known Error Database**: Subset of knowledge base documenting problems and workarounds

**Knowledge Article Types**:
- How-to guides
- Troubleshooting procedures
- FAQs
- Best practices
- Runbooks

## Knowledge Lifecycle

1. **Create**: Capture knowledge from incidents, problems, projects
2. **Review**: Validate accuracy and completeness
3. **Approve**: Authorize for publication
4. **Publish**: Make available to intended audience
5. **Use**: Reference during incident/request resolution
6. **Update**: Keep current as systems change
7. **Retire**: Archive obsolete articles

## Do's

✅ Make knowledge easily searchable
✅ Reward knowledge creation and sharing
✅ Integrate knowledge base with ITSM tools
✅ Track article usage and feedback
✅ Keep articles current
✅ Use simple, clear language

## Don'ts

❌ Hoard knowledge in individual silos
❌ Let knowledge base become outdated
❌ Make knowledge hard to find
❌ Publish unreviewed or inaccurate information

## Key Metrics

- Knowledge article usage (views, search hits)
- Article accuracy (user ratings, feedback)
- Knowledge reuse rate (% incidents resolved using existing articles)
- Time to create and publish articles
